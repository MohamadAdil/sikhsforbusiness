What is

Sikhs for business

Sikhs for Business was initiated in June 2024 from Ludhiana as a visionary project to empower and connect Sikh entrepreneurs, small and medium business owners, and professionals across the globe. It began with the goal of creating a strong, value-based networking platform where like-minded individuals could come together, support each other’s ventures, and grow collectively. What started as a small effort to build community collaboration quickly evolved into a powerful ecosystem, enabling members to share resources, exchange business opportunities, and receive guidance. With several operational chapters in different cities, Sikhs for Business continues to grow as a vibrant network that promotes economic development and collective prosperity.

Open forums

Open Forum is a unique networking event where business and spirituality come together. What makes it special is its peaceful setting—it is held in a local Gurudwara Sahib, combining professional growth with cultural and spiritual values. The event is well-organized and brings together around 100–120 entrepreneurs for a 150-minute networking session.

Closed forums

Closed Forum is a structured networking platform with fixed members, designed to foster deep connections, focused referrals, and long-term collaboration among entrepreneurs. Focused Networking with Trusted Members as the name suggests.
